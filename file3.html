THE SPMS PURPOSE

>Help students manage their portfolios – so they can store and showcase their projects easily and neatly.

> Showcase achievements and accomplishments – such as certificates, rewards, or projects that earned high grades.

>Enhance professionalism – giving students a clear interface that helps them present their skills in a professional way.

>Facilitate communication – providing students with a way to connect with teachers or others through the Contact page.

> Increase visibility and student recognition – ensuring their projects are clearly seen by others (teachers, potential employers, or peers).

THE TARGET AUDIENCE

>Students – they will use the system to manage their portfolios, showcase their projects, achievements, and certificates.

> Teachers/Educators – they will have an easy way to review a student’s progress, projects, and how they present themselves professionally.

> Employers/Recruiters – they will see a student’s portfolio clearly, helping them assess the student’s skills and select them for jobs or internships.

THE BENEFITS OF SPMS(Student Poltifolio Managment System)

>Organized Student Portfolios – it helps students keep their projects, achievements, and certificates in one clear, accessible place.

>Professional Presentation – the system provides a clean interface that allows students to showcase their skills in a polished, professional way.

>Improved Communication & Visibility – with a contact section and portfolio sharing, students can easily connect with teachers, peers, or potential employers, making their work more visible.

OUR FUTURE VISION

> .Be a model platform for students :– helping each student showcase their projects professionally and build their personal brand.

> .Expand student visibility – :ensuring their projects are clearly seen by teachers, peers, and potential employers.
